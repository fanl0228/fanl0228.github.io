Long Fan is currently a Lecturer at the School of Computer Science, Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT). He received his Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University (NJU), advised by Prof. Lei Xie. From 2024 to 2025, he was a Visiting Researcher at the College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Jun Luo.

His research interests lie in the intersection of Pervasive Computing, Wireless Sensing and Mobile Computing.
